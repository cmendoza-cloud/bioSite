Interview Questions

About Me Hobbies Interview Questions
1. What inspired you to become a wrestler?
“I had a very humble childhood; resources were not always available and to help my family I decided to use my passion for wrestling to help with the financial burdens.”
2. How did you start wrestling?
“The wrestling coach visited my school, and he invited the students to participate in a training session and told us of all the benefits of the sport if we managed to qualify for Nationals.”
3. What was your favorite match that you participated in and why?
“My favorite match was in August of 2012; I was the underdog of the match competing against the capital’s favorite. What made this my favorite match was that I was injured and still won my match."
4. What was the most challenging part of your training regimen?
“Cutting weight was always the most challenging part of my training. I had to do all my training exercises with low energy because of my strict diet. Training was still not enough to cut weight I would have to watch my calorie intake and do intense cardio after practice.”
5. How did you prepare mentally and physically before a match?
“Physically by light sparring before every match and warm-ups to ensure my body was ready. Mentally before every match, I would listen to rock music to keep me motivated for the match, one of my favorites was Psychosocial by Slipknot.”
6. Do you have a favorite wrestler or role model that inspires you?
“My favorite wrestler is Henry Cejudo, he was the youngest wrestler to win the Olympics for the United States and now he is a professional UFC fighter.”
7. Who has been your biggest influence or mentor in wrestling?
“That would be my coach Alexander Molina, he always pushed me to be the best I could be.”
8. What are some of your proudest accomplishments in your wrestling career?
“Having been able to travel to other countries with the Salvadorian National Team and to have represented El Salvador at a national level.”
9. What advice would you give to someone aspiring to begin wrestling?
“Do not be so hard on yourself if you do not compete well/win your first matches and keep trying and don’t give up. With discipline and the right mindset, you will accomplish your goals.”
10. Do you have any other goals or hobbies beyond wrestling?
“Yes, I have picked up a passion for photography. I can capture moments through the lens that allows me to express my creativity and share stories through a different medium.”
